How to Make a Chocolate Cake!

What kind of cake will you be making?

The cake you will be making will be a Hershey chocolate cake. It will, of course, be a chocolate cake covered in chocolate buttercream frosting. The cake itself will be nice and moist and is perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients, or What You Will Need:

The cake and the frosting require different types of ingedients, so they will be listed separately. Here are the ingredients for the cake:

  • 2 cups of white sugar
  • 1 and 3/4 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 of a cup of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons of baking soda
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/2 of a cup of vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of boiling water

Ingredients needed for the frosting:

  • 3/4 of a cup of unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 5 and 1/3 cups of confectioners' sugar
  • 1 and 1/2 cups of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2/3 of a cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
